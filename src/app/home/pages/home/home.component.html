Tutorial of Features

Creating a Wardrobe (Group of outfits)

Wardrobes are groups of outfits that follow a theme.

Examples are Nightfalls, PVP, "Contraverse Hold" builds, etc.

A wardrobe will need to be created before storing outfits.

Creating an Outfit (Stored set of Equipment)

An outfit is a recorded set of equipment.

An outfit stores sub-class, weapons, armor, and ghost items.

Clicking the capture button will record the currently equipped items into an outfit.

Vault Transfer Setting

Normally, equipment is transfered to-and-from the character's inventory.

This setting will allow retrieving equipment from the vault if they are not in the inventory.

Items being unequipped will be sent to the vault to keep the inventory light.
