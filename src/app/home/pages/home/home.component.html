It's a hobby-site/project started during stay-at-home quarantine.

Bungie's Companion Apis are used to swap sets of equipment at a time.

The swaps can occur off-line, in orbit, the tower, and other safe spaces.

An account with Bungie will be needed to use this feature.

Tutorial of Features

Creating a Wardrobe (Group of outfits)

Wardrobes are groups of outfits that follow a theme.

Examples are Nightfalls, PVP, "Contraverse" builds, etc.

A wardrobe will need to be created before storing outfits.

Creating an Outfit (Stored set of Equipment)

An outfit is a set of equipment that can be swapped to.

An outfit stores sub-class, weapons, armor, and ghost items.

Clicking the capture button will record the currently equipped items into an outfit.

Vault Transfer Setting

Normally, equipment is transfered to/from the character's inventory.

This setting will allow retrieving from the vault if items are not in the inventory.

Items being unequipped will be sent to the vault to keep the inventory light.
